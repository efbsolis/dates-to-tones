Calendar to tone harmonies

Sound off Sound on

This is a small app that produces harmonies based on dates of the year. It treats a year like a wheel that begins in January and rolls back through December, and finds out, clockwise and counterclockwise, how many days separate these two dates from either side. These are turned into ratios by comparing their relative sizes, and these ratios become notes.

One particularly good sounding pair of dates you can try out is 07/03/1974 and 11/02/1985.
